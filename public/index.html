About us

We are a Pretoria-based company that specialises in sharpening:

Our mission is to provide reliable, fast, precision sharpening with exceptional customer service. By providing you with high-quality sharpening in a fraction of the time, we help to increase the efficiency and success of your business - sharpening your potential.

After extensive research, we have established that the majority of Restaurants etc. are paying exorbitant prices for brand new utensils and instruments. We have as established that the majority of owners have been investing in brand new utensils and instruments as opposed to having the old utensils and instruments just sharpened at less than half the price or more.

Example businesses that could benefit from our services

  • Hunting Safaris
  • Restaurants
  • Catering
  • Hotels

  • OR Home Use

Collection and Delivery and or done on premises for your convenience
